About

👨‍🏫 A short story of me

Hello there! Thank you for visiting my digital workspace. I am William Fabian Setiadi, a dedicated Business Information Systems student at ISTTS based in Surabaya. I have a strong passion for Fullstack and Front-End development. I build solid programming foundations using Java, SQL, HTML, and CSS. I focus on creating structured, system-driven environments for every web application I develop. I also actively manage my code utilizing tools like NetBeans and GitHub to ensure project reliability from day one.


I apply my technical skills directly in academic leadership roles. I actively serve as a Laboratory Assistant for the ACS and MAD courses. This position trains me to mentor peers, prepare complex technical tests, and solve problems efficiently. I thrive in collaborative environments and value clear communication. I always ensure my code is clean, scalable, and easy for other developers to maintain.


Discipline and endurance form my core work ethic. I regularly train for long-distance running using heart rate zone methods and maintain a consistent weekend swimming routine. I apply this level of consistency and stamina to complex programming challenges. I adapt to new technologies with enthusiasm and think analytically to build optimal solutions. I am excited to bring my dedication to a forward-thinking team. Let us build impactful digital experiences together!
